ABOUT ADETIVE



Every solution has to start with a problem. Our story begins with the reasons why mid-level companies and rising enterprises cannot compete with the major brands. It’s because of their advertising and marketing. So we’re on a quest to find a solution to this global business dilemma. At this time, we discovered that there is one thing in Marketing that is far superior to money. That is good content and context with more creativity.


So, by integrating all three, we built our first campaign, for which we spent a small budget but had a massive response. That was our first success, and we discovered that the creative campaign is the only approach to assist all levels of businesses for all budgets. So finally, Adetive now stands out in the industry as a Business strategic campaign firm.
